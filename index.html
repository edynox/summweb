How it works

It's simple.

SummApp processes your calls into a neat collection of extracted information and suggested actions that you can view, edit, search for and share.

Make a call

Talk about your business

Share relevant data with others

Get the summary

After the call, you will see the extracted information and suggested actions

Create an event

From the summary, you can add an event with all relevant information to your calendar, share it or invoke other actions
